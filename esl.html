Student Resources

Raz Kids is a website where students can read interactive books and audio books online anytime, anywhere! eQuizzes test students' comprehension and provides the teacher with progress reports for each student.

Epic! is the leading digitial library for students 12 and under. Here the student's can log in with their class code and they will have access to many different books.

Class Dojo is a site where students, teachers, and parents can connect and build an amazing community! Students can add photos and videos to their portfolios to showcase and share their learning. Parents can see all their students' hard work.

Google Classroom is an online classroom where students can access their assignments. Parents can check their students' page to make sure they're getting their work done.
