Program Expectations

Classroom Rules

  • Come to class prepared always.
  • Follow directions the first time given.
  • Be respectful towards the teacher and classmates at all times.
  • Study and practice daily.
  • Hand in all assignments on time.
  • Keep track of grades, progress, and behavior.
  • Have fun!!!!

Behavior Expectations

If the student breaks the rules:

  • The student will receive two warnings.
  • After the two warnings, the teacher will establish a consequence, including lunch detention.
  • If the behavior continued, the teacher will contact parents, and the consequence would possibly include an afternoon detention.
